Small uppercase tag for day badges, "Most Popular" flags, and status pills.

```jsx
<Badge variant="gold">Weekday 2PM CST</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline">Weekend 2PM CST</Badge>
<Badge variant="red">Most Popular</Badge>
```

Variants: `gold` (solid), `outline` (gold border), `red` (action), `ghost` (faint gold).
