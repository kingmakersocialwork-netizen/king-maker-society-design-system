Sales Scripts
💬

Cold Outreach

First message to someone who doesn't know us. Short, genuine, about them.

Yo [Name], came across your page and I fw the content. You training [art]? Asking because we work with a lot of fighters who are trying to grow their brand and stay accountable. Might be something worth looking into depending on what you're working on.

🔥

Comment Trigger ("KING")

They commented KING on a post. Hit them while the energy is there.

Saw you dropped "KING" on the post. Appreciate that. What are you training right now? Trying to get a feel for where you're at before I send you anything.

📋

Questionnaire Send

Use this after you've built context on who they are and what they want.

Before I go into detail I want to make sure I point you in the right direction. I have a quick form that takes 2 minutes. It helps us understand where you're at so we're not just throwing stuff at you. [Questionnaire Link] Fill that out and I'll follow up personally based on your answers.

💰

Payment Link Send

After they're sold. Keep it clean — no over-explaining.

Based on everything you told me, the [Basic / VIP] membership is the right move. It's $[30/100]/month, cancel anytime, no contracts. Here's the link: [Payment Link] Once you're in I'll personally make sure you get set up and introduced to the group.

🔄

Follow-Up (No Response)

They went quiet. One clean follow-up. Don't chase.

Hey just circling back. No pressure at all. If the timing isn't right that's fully cool. Just didn't want to leave you hanging if you had questions. Either way keep training.

🤔

"I Need To Think About It"

Find out what's actually holding them back.

Totally fair. What part are you weighing up? Is it the timing, the price, or just not sure if it's the right fit? Asking because I want to give you an honest answer, not just sell you on it.
