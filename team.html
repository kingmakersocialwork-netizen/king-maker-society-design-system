Sales Lead Flow SOP

📈 Step-By-Step Lead Process

  1. 1Identify the lead. Could be a follower, commenter, someone who liked a story, a martial artist found through search. Log their name and Instagram handle in the tracking sheet.
  2. 2Send the first DM. Use the Cold Outreach or Warm Outreach script. Keep it short. About them. Ask one question only.
  3. 3Build the conversation. Ask about their martial art, their goals, what they're working on, what's holding them back. Listen more than you pitch.
  4. 4Send the questionnaire. Once you have enough context, send the form link. Explain it takes 2 minutes and it helps personalize what you send them.
  5. 5Review their answers. Identify their biggest pain point. Build your pitch around that specific thing — not a generic pitch.
  6. 6Present the membership. Tie it directly to their pain point. "You said X — that's exactly what the [Basic/VIP] membership is built for." Start with Basic unless VIP is clearly the right fit.
  7. 7Handle objections. Use the objection scripts. Don't cave on price. Focus on value. If they need time, give them time but stay in contact.
  8. 8Send payment link. Once they're ready, send clean with no over-explaining. Confirm they received it. Follow up in 24 hours if no action.
  9. 9Onboard them. As soon as payment clears, send the welcome DM, add them to the group, introduce them, and set their first accountability goal.
  10. 10Update the tracking sheet. Mark as Joined. Note what worked. Use their story as future social proof content.
