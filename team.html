Sales Scripts
💬

Cold Outreach

First message to someone who doesn't know us. Short, genuine, about them.

Yo [Name], came across your page and I fw the content. You training [art]? Asking because we work with a lot of fighters who are trying to grow their brand and stay accountable. Might be something worth looking into depending on what you're working on.

🔥

Comment Trigger ("KING")

They commented KING on a post. Hit them while the energy is there.

Saw you dropped "KING" on the post. Appreciate that. What are you training right now? Trying to get a feel for where you're at before I send you anything.

👥

New Follower DM

They just followed the page. Strike while it's warm.

Hey [Name], appreciate the follow. You a martial artist? Just want to put a face to the page before anything else.

📋

Questionnaire Send

Use after you've built context on who they are and what they want.

Before I go into detail I want to make sure I point you in the right direction. I have a quick form that takes 2 minutes. It helps us understand where you're at so we're not just throwing stuff at you. [Questionnaire Link] Fill that out and I'll follow up personally based on your answers.

💰

Payment Link Send

After they're sold. Keep it clean — no over-explaining.

Based on everything you told me, the [Basic / VIP] membership is the right move. It's $[30/100]/month, cancel anytime, no contracts. Here's the link: [Payment Link] Once you're in I'll personally make sure you get set up and introduced to the group.

🔄

Follow-Up (No Response)

They went quiet. One clean follow-up. Don't chase.

Hey just circling back. No pressure at all. If the timing isn't right that's fully cool. Just didn't want to leave you hanging if you had questions. Either way keep training.

🤔

"I Need To Think About It"

Find out what's actually holding them back.

Totally fair. What part are you weighing up? Is it the timing, the price, or just not sure if it's the right fit? Asking because I want to give you an honest answer, not just sell you on it.

💸

"I Can't Afford It"

Don't lower the price. Understand what they value.

Respect that. $30 is real money depending on the situation. What's the main thing you're trying to fix right now — accountability, content, mindset, or network? I want to make sure it's worth it before I push you toward anything.

👥

"Already In A Group"

They have another community. Differentiate, don't dismiss.

Respect that. Most groups are either info-heavy or full of people who aren't actually moving. King Maker Society is different because it's specifically for martial artists who are trying to build something — a brand, a career, a mindset. Not just consume content. What's the main thing your current group is helping you with?

Sales Lead Flow SOP

📈 Step-By-Step Lead Process

  1. 1Identify the lead. Could be a follower, commenter, someone who liked a story, a martial artist found through search. Log their name and Instagram handle in the tracking sheet.
  2. 2Send the first DM. Use the Cold Outreach or Warm Outreach script. Keep it short. About them. Ask one question only.
  3. 3Build the conversation. Ask about their martial art, their goals, what they're working on, what's holding them back. Listen more than you pitch.
  4. 4Send the questionnaire. Once you have enough context, send the form link. Explain it takes 2 minutes and it helps personalize what you send them.
  5. 5Review their answers. Identify their biggest pain point. Build your pitch around that specific thing — not a generic pitch.
  6. 6Present the membership. Tie it directly to their pain point. "You said X — that's exactly what the [Basic/VIP] membership is built for." Start with Basic unless VIP is clearly the right fit.
  7. 7Handle objections. Use the objection scripts. Don't cave on price. Focus on value. If they need time, give them time but stay in contact.
  8. 8Send payment link. Once they're ready, send clean with no over-explaining. Confirm they received it. Follow up in 24 hours if no action.
  9. 9Onboard them. As soon as payment clears, send the welcome DM, add them to the group, introduce them, and set their first accountability goal.
  10. 10Update the tracking sheet. Mark as Joined. Note what worked. Use their story as future social proof content.
