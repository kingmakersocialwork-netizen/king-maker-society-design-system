What You Get

Mentorship That Actually Works

Most people never get access to someone who's actually done it. Inside King Maker Society, that changes.

01 🏆

Live Group Calls

Sit in on live Zoom discussions with mentors every week. Ask questions, get real answers, and learn alongside your brothers.

02 💬

Direct Chat Access

Ask mentors directly inside the community chat. No gatekeeping, no waiting weeks for a reply — real access to real people.

03 ⚡

Fight IQ Sessions

Film review, game planning, mental approach, and the technical details that separate fighters who grow from those who plateau.

04 👑

Mindset Coaching

The mental side of martial arts is where most people fall short. Mentors address the inner work — confidence, pressure, and discipline.

05 📈

Personal Brand & Exposure

Build your name, grow your content, and position yourself for sponsors and opportunities — with guidance from people who've done it.

06 👥

Brotherhood Accountability

Mentors hold you to a standard. They check in, push back when needed, and celebrate your wins alongside the whole community.
