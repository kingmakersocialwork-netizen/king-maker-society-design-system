Sharp, uppercase King Maker Society button — use red `primary` for commit/Join actions, gold variants for secondary CTAs and trials.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg">Join The Brotherhood — $30/mo</Button>
<Button variant="outline" href="https://example.com">Try Free For 7 Days</Button>
<Button variant="gold">Start My Free Trial</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">Book A Call</Button>
```

Variants: `primary` (red, action only), `outline` (gold border), `gold` (solid gold on dark), `ghost` (nav). Sizes: `sm` / `md` / `lg`. Pass `href` to render an anchor. All lift -2px on hover.
