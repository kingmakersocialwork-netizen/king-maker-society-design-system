The System

How We Keep You Moving

No motivation. No hype. A real weekly structure that keeps every brother locked in — start strong, stay honest, reset and repeat.

Mon Monday Check-In
  • What is your main goal this week?
  • What are you training today?
  • What are you avoiding that you need to face?
  • What content are you posting this week?
  • What is your biggest obstacle right now?
Fri Friday Check-Out
  • Did you hit your goal for the week?
  • What is one win from this week?
  • What is one thing you need to fix this week?
  • What habit are you rebuilding?
  • How are your energy levels right now?
Sun Sunday Reset
  • What does next week look like?
  • What are your three priorities?
  • What training sessions are locked in?
  • What do you need to let go of?
  • Are you showing up for yourself?
